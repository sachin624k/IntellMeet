# IntellMeet

## AI-Powered Enterprise Meeting & Collaboration Platform

---

## Project Overview

IntellMeet is a full-stack enterprise meeting and collaboration platform that enables teams to conduct secure video meetings, collaborate in real-time, generate AI-powered meeting summaries, manage tasks, and track meeting history.

The project is being built using the MERN Stack with additional technologies like Socket.io, WebRTC, OpenAI, and Cloudinary.

---

## Problem Statement

Most online meetings end without proper documentation.

People forget action items.

Meeting recordings become difficult to search.

There is no centralized collaboration platform combining meetings, AI summaries, tasks, and chat.

---

## Our Solution

IntellMeet provides

- Secure Authentication
- Video Meetings
- Team Chat
- AI Meeting Summaries
- Task Management
- Meeting History
- Analytics Dashboard
